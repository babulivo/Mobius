Endless possibilities
in your hands.
EXPLORE Möbius
In 2023, our team of engineers and designers faced the limitations of traditional form factors when working on a new computer mouse project. We wanted to create a mouse that was not only ergonomic and functional, but could also adapt to individual user needs.
The name of the mouse is inspired by the Mobius strip, an infinite surface with one side. This is also the case with our product: by customizing the mouse, each user creates one of an infinite number of variations of the same mouse.
overview
PAW3395 Sensor
The SkyPAD Möbius is equipped with the PixArt PAW3395. According to specifications, the 3395 is capable of up to 26,000 CPI, as well as a maximum tracking speed of 650 IPS, which equals 16.51 m/s. Out of the box, five pre-defined CPI steps are available: 400, 800, 1600, 3200, and 5000.
Pure Teflon Feets
Virgin PTFE mouse feet offer less friction, are smoother, and last longer than OEM feet that have mixed combinations of polymers. Once you experience the unmatched smoothness, control, and responsiveness of pure Teflon feet, you'll never settle for anything less.
Seperate Panels
With the detachable panel system, this mouse transcends the limitations of a single design. It becomes a versatile tool that adapts to your needs and evolves with your style, offering unmatched customization and long-lasting value.
Only 52 grams
The ultra-lightweight design minimizes inertia, enabling lightning-fast flicks and effortless glides across your mousepad. Say goodbye to fatigue and experience flawless control.
